LRAEval 
1.0.0

The service-operation collection was extracted from real service offerings, in order to represent the variety in terms of design patterns and resources found in real environments. We selected the domain of scholarly APIs, and assisted by a domain expert, we used a subset of the list of APIs published by the University of Alberta Libraries. The APIs were organized using Swagger UI, containing the following service providers: arXiv, Australian National Data Service (ANDS), AltMetric, Connecting Repositories System (CORE), Crossref, Elsevier, IEEEXplore, Mendeley, Nature Blogs, National Science Foundation (NSF), ORCID, PatentsView, and PubMed E-Utilities. The test collection of services is described by the natural language documentation, in addition to their formal semantic descriptions, in order to allow the reuse in other approaches, and avoid any bias that may be introduced by the abstraction process of creating the formal descriptions.

Note: The responses returned by the API console are illustrative. They don't correspond to an actual request to the specified endpoint.

Note: For better visualization of the SVG images in the results section, use Chrome as the browser.

Questions

1. Which patents have been assigned to a person?
 In: Person name.
 Out: Country, date, number, type, assignee's name, title.

2. Which awards have been allocated to an institution?
 In: Institution name.
 Out: Award title, amount*, date*, person name, and agency name.

3. Which awards have been allocated to a person?
 In: Person name, ORCID id*.
 Out: Award title, amount*, date*, and agency name.

4. Which are the publications of an person?
 In: Person name, ORCID id*, year range*.
 Out: Publication title, date, DOI*, abstract*, link*, collection name (journal).

5. Which articles have been published in a specific venue?
 In: Venue name, date range*.
 Out: Same as 4.

6a/b. Which/How many publications have cited a publication?
 In: Publication title.
 Out: Citing publication title, date, authors, and venue name / Citation count.

7a/b. Which/How many publications have cited the publications of an author?
 In: Author name, citing publication date*.
 Out: Same as 6a/b.

8. Which are the coauthors of a person?
 In: Person name.
 Out: Coauthor name, affiliation*, and publication title.

* Optional attributes.
